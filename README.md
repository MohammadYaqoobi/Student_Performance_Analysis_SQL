## üìÇ Repository Structure & Analysis
This repository contains 4 SQL scripts, answering specific managerial questions:

### 1. Risk Behavior Analysis
*   **File:** `Query 1 Impact of High Risk Behavior (Alcohol & Health) on Final Grades`
*   **Objective:** Analyzing how alcohol consumption and health status correlate with final grades.
*   **Insight:** Identifying students who need immediate counseling due to high-risk behaviors.

### 2. Effectiveness of Support Systems
*   **File:** `Query 2 Effectiveness of Educational Support Systems`
*   **Objective:** Evaluating whether paid classes and school support are actually improving student grades (Return on Investment analysis).
*   **Insight:** Comparison of grade improvement between supported and non-supported students.

### 3. Family Dynamics (Social Psychology)
*   **File:** `Query 3 Correlation between Family Stability and Academic Performance`
*   **Objective:** Investigating the impact of parental cohabitation status (Pstatus) and family relationship quality on study time and grades.
*   **Insight:** Validating the correlation between a stable home environment and academic focus.

### 4. Predictive Assessment
*   **File:** `Query 4 Predicting Failure based on Past Failures and First Period Grade`
*   **Objective:** Creating logic to predict future failure based on 1st-period grades and past failures.
*   **Insight:** Helping school administrators identify "At-Risk" students early in the semester.
